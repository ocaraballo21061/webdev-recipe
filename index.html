HOW TO MAKE SLURP JUICE FROM EPIC GAMES FORTNITE

RECIPIE

Before Cooking Make Sure You Have:

  • A Large Soda Bottle
  • 2 Lithium Batteries
  • 1 Full Pack of Sudaphed or Whatever That Stuff is
  • Two Types of Industial Acids, One of Them Being Muriatic Acid and The Other I forgot...
  • Some other Dangerous Chemical Found in Strong Cleaning Supplies

PROCEDURE:

  1. First, Cut open the 2 lithium batteries and pour any excreting liquids into the soda bottle.
  2. Next, pour the Sudaphed, Industrial Acids and Strong Cleaning Supplies into to bottle and mix.
  3. Heat and mix until glowing
  4. Once glowing, pour mountain dew until the bottle is filled.
  5. Chill the mixture and make sure to shake the mix well before Drinking
  6. Enjoy!
